Physics For All

Physics is a central science that enables students to explore topics such as mechanics, electricity, and magnetism. As most schools do not expose students to physics, we aim to provide students with the resources and mentorship necessary to break into the Physics field.

Competition and Olympiads

We will be administering the F=MA exam and the USAPhO exams during the school year. Additionally, we will hold practice rounds and school-wide competitions to provide students with rigourous competitive experience in Physics.

Guest Speaker Events

Students will gain the unique opportunity to learn from industry experts and professionals in fields such as biophysics and computational physics.
